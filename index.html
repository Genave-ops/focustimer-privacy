Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

FocusTimer ("we", "our", or "the app") is a productivity timer application built by genave13. This privacy policy explains how FocusTimer handles your information.

In short: FocusTimer does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All your data stays on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

FocusTimer does not collect, store on external servers, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Photos, contacts, or calendar data

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

FocusTimer stores the following data exclusively on your device using local storage to provide its core functionality:

  • Timer settings: Your preferred focus duration, break durations, and timer preferences.
  • Session history: Records of your completed focus sessions, including task labels you enter, session duration, and timestamps. A maximum of 1,000 sessions are retained.

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party. If you uninstall the app or clear the app's data, all locally stored information is permanently deleted.

Permissions

FocusTimer requests only the minimum permissions necessary:

  • Vibration: Used to provide haptic feedback when timers complete and during interactions.
  • Notifications: Used to alert you when a focus or break session is complete. All notifications are generated locally on your device; no push notification service is used.
  • Foreground service: Used to keep the timer running accurately when the app is in the background.

Third-Party Services

FocusTimer does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not make network requests to external servers.

Children's Privacy

FocusTimer does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages as it operates entirely offline and stores no personal data externally.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
